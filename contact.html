Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Nairobi takes 2-3 business days. For other regions in Kenya, delivery takes 3-5 business days. For Buy For Me services from the US, delivery takes approximately 3 weeks.

Do you offer warranty on products?

Yes, most of our products come with a warranty period ranging from 3 months to 1 year depending on the product. Please check individual product pages for specific warranty information.

Can I return a product?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for defective or damaged products. The product must be in its original condition with all packaging and accessories. Custom orders and Buy For Me items may have different return policies.

How does the Buy For Me service work?

Simply find the product you want from any US website, use our calculator to get the total cost in KES, then place your order via WhatsApp. We handle everything from purchase to delivery to your doorstep in Kenya.
