Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by adding products to your cart and proceeding to checkout, or via WhatsApp by sending us the products you're interested in. For Buy For Me services, use the calculator on our services page and contact us via WhatsApp with the product details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash on delivery (for select areas in Nairobi). For larger orders or Buy For Me services, we may require a deposit with the balance payable before delivery.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placement by contacting us via WhatsApp or email. After this period, orders may already be in processing and cannot be changed.

Do you offer installment payments?

Currently, we don't offer formal installment plans. However, for returning customers or larger orders, we may arrange flexible payment options on a case-by-case basis. Contact us to discuss your needs.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location:
- Nairobi: 2-3 business days
- Other major towns: 3-5 business days
- Remote areas: 5-7 business days
- Buy For Me service: Approximately 3 weeks from order confirmation

Do you deliver outside Nairobi?

Yes, we deliver nationwide across Kenya. Delivery costs and times may vary depending on your location. Contact us for specific delivery information to your area.

Can I track my order?

Yes, we provide tracking information for all orders. Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS or WhatsApp that you can use to monitor your delivery status.

What are your delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary based on your location and order size:
- Nairobi: KES 200-500
- Other areas: KES 300-800
- Free delivery for orders over KES 5,000 within Nairobi
Contact us for exact delivery costs to your location.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for defective or damaged products. The product must be in its original condition with all packaging and accessories. Custom orders and Buy For Me items may have different return policies.

Do your products come with warranty?

Yes, most products come with warranties ranging from 3 months to 1 year depending on the product type. Check individual product pages for specific warranty information. Warranty covers manufacturing defects but not damage from misuse.

How do I make a warranty claim?

Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your order details, product issue, and photos/videos of the problem. We'll guide you through the warranty claim process, which may include repair, replacement, or refund depending on the situation.

What is not covered by warranty?

Warranty does not cover:
- Damage from accidents, drops, or liquid exposure
- Normal wear and tear
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Use with incompatible accessories
- Cosmetic damage from normal use

Products & Services

How does the Buy For Me service work?

Our Buy For Me service allows you to purchase products from US websites and have them delivered to Kenya. Simply:
1. Find the product you want on any US website
2. Use our calculator to get the total cost in KES
3. Contact us via WhatsApp with the product link
4. Make payment and we handle everything else
Delivery takes approximately 3 weeks.

Are your products authentic?

Yes, all our products are 100% authentic. We source directly from authorized distributors and reputable suppliers. We stand behind the quality and authenticity of every product we sell.

Do you offer bulk/wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders and wholesale customers. Contact us with your requirements and quantity needs, and we'll provide you with competitive wholesale pricing.

Can I request a product that's not on your website?

Absolutely! If you're looking for a specific product that's not listed on our website, contact us with the details. We can source most tech products and accessories through our network of suppliers.
